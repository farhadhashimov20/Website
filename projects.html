Water Treatment Plant

The project encompasses the automation, electrical wiring, and paneling of a Water Treatment Plant. The hardware employed includes Siemens LOGO and GMTCNT PLCs, while the Operator Station/HMI is equipped with a GMTCNT HMI. The project integrates two distinct water treatment technologies, involving a variety of components such as filters, pumps, adhesives, mixers, and blowers.

In the first stage of the process, commercial water is treated using blowers and subsequently filtered, with the filters being cleaned by UV light. The second stage focuses on the chemical treatment of the water, which includes regulating acidity and other chemical parameters, ensuring that the wastewater is made safe for discharge into the river.
