Cement Filling Station

Duration: Jun 2023 - Aug 2023

Associated Companies: Avtomatika Az LLC, Vien LLC

Customer Company: Holcim Azerbaijan OJSC

The Holcim OJSC industrial automation project in Cabrayil involved the establishment of a cement filling station equipped with advanced screw conveyors and an elevator system. To ensure precise level measurement, discrete rotating level sensors were implemented in the facility. The heart of the project's control system relied on Siemens PLC technology, seamlessly integrated with the WinCC SCADA system, facilitating real-time monitoring and efficient control of the entire cement filling process.
